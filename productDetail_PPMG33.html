Salient Feature

It is Flange Mounting single stage reduction Worm Gear DC Permanent Magnet Motor Which delivers Moderate Torque at any set speed. Speed of Armature can be varied by adjusting Armature voltage. The Motor shaft employs grease lubricated Ball Bearing on both sides. Worm is made out of special steel, Hardened and Ground. Worm Wheel is made out of Cast Bronze lubricate with grease. Shaft is supported by Ball Bearing on both side. Motor body, Gearbox are protected against corrosion by pre-treatment and painted
